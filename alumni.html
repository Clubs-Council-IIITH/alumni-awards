Starting from the 25th anniversary of the institute in 2023, IIIT-H is proud to honor a select group of prestigious alumni for their professional achievements and outstanding service to the institute in the following award categories:

Distinguished Alumni Award for outstanding contributions to the society at large

Outstanding Service Award for outstanding service to Institute/Alumni network

Criteria For Nomination

Nominator

  • Should be an Alumni of iiit Hyderabad
  • Should be 5+ years since graduation or current/past faculty of institute
  • Can nominate two nominations maximum per year

    • Nominee

  • Should be an Alumni of iiit Hyderabad
  • Should be 5+ years since graduation

    • Note

  • Self-nomination is not allowed
  • Any employee of IIITH or were in the recent past (past 7 years) would not be eligible for this nomination
  • Governing Council Alumni members would not be eligible during their current tenure (would be eligible once done with tenure)

    • Procedure to Nominate

    Please fill in the following form to nominate. The form will include Nominee as well as Nominator details as below:

    Nominee Details

    Name of nominee, Current work organization, Designation, Brief details of nature of work, Program and years alumni was associated with IIIT, Linkedin link, Website if present, Clippings or youtube links

    Nominator Details

    Name, Current work organization, Designation, Program (if alumni) and years (alumni and faculty) associated with IIIT.

    Submission

    1-page writeup justifying your nomination of this alumni for the distinguished alumnus award.

    Zip file of the submission can be uploaded.
